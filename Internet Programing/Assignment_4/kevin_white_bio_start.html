About Me

My name is Kevin White, and I am currently studying at Texas A&M University-Corpus Christi. I expect to graduate in May 2024 with a bachelor's in Computer Science with a concentration in Cyber Security. I am the oldest of four boys: trying to be the best role model I can for my brothers. My first introduction to computer science was my first year of high school when I made a simple clicker game available on my phone. It was amazing being able to send and share this project. It was unlike anything I had before; since then, I've been in love with computer science. After graduating from High School in 2024, I went to college at TAMU-CC. During my second year at TAMU-CC, I entered my first Cyber Security competition to test the waters. My team and I got first place! The victory motivated and pushed me to try and do the best I could within the field. I am Now The Vice President of the Cyber Security Club.

I can't wait to join the workforce and make a difference so I can help the people around me. My goal is to push the boundaries of computer science and create something innovative.

This website is an archive of some of my accomplishments and proud moments in my life.
